Workshop Structure

How we typically structure AI adoption journeys.

We usually move from understanding and experimentation into team-level adoption and then into tool or workflow deployment.

01

AI Foundations

For broad team orientation

Introductory sessions that help teams understand the AI landscape, common use cases, responsible adoption, and practical everyday usage.

  • AI basics and current landscape
  • Responsible and safe usage
  • Live demos across work contexts
  • Prompting and experimentation

02

Team Deep Dives

For function-specific use cases

Focused workshops for teams like marketing, finance, sales, leadership, education, or program teams with examples that feel immediately relevant.

  • Department-specific use cases
  • Role-based workflows
  • Hands-on guided practice
  • Tool selection for the team

03

Build and Deployment Sprints

For implementation and prototypes

Sessions that move beyond training into actual deployment, prototype creation, internal copilots, automations, and tailored AI tools.

  • Prototype creation
  • Workflow and automation design
  • Knowledge tool setup
  • Deployment-oriented guidance

How the workshops are run

  • Practical and adoption-led, not abstract

  • Designed for real workflows and real teams
  • Blended with live demos, practice, and discussion
