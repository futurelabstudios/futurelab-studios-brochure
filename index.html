Areas of Strength

What our workshops typically look like and what they are designed to unlock.

The structure below keeps the offering easy to understand: the workshop formats, the way they are delivered, and the tools or systems we commonly cover or build around.

01

AI Workshops

We run hands-on workshops that help teams understand the AI landscape, identify use cases, and start using AI safely and productively in their day-to-day work.

02

AI Tool Building

We also work with organizations to build AI tools, copilots, and workflows that move from training into live implementation.

AI Essentials

For all team members

3 sessions • 2 hours each

Overview of the AI landscape and latest trends. Outcome: confidence and safe everyday AI use.

  • Landscape and trends
  • Everyday productivity tool walkthrough
  • Cross-department use cases
  • Responsible AI and data privacy

AI Deep Dive

For specific teams

4 sessions • 90 mins each

Built for functions like marketing, finance, sales, and operations. Outcome: team-level adoption and efficiency.

  • Department-specific demos
  • Quick wins for immediate adoption
  • Advanced prompt engineering
  • Guided participant practice

AI Builders

For teams ready to build

Workshop + build sprint

For teams that want to move from learning into prototypes and working tools.

  • Hackathon-style sessions
  • Prototype real solutions
  • Automation and API workflows
  • AI safety and security practices

How the workshops feel

  • Jargon-free, plain-English learning
  • Tailored to the organization and team context
