Workshop Structure

Our AI enablement offerings.

We usually move from broad AI confidence into department-level adoption and then into builder sprints for teams ready to create working tools.

AI Essentials

3 sessions • 2 hours each

All team members — mixed cohorts and leadership

  • Overview of the AI landscape and headline trends relevant to your sector
  • Everyday productivity tools with live walkthroughs teams can repeat
  • Cross-department use cases so each function sees concrete relevance
  • Data privacy and responsible AI aligned to how your organization works
  • Knowledge handouts and prompt libraries for sustained use after training

Outcome • Confidence and safe, everyday AI use

AI Deep Dive

4 sessions • 90 minutes each

Specific teams — marketing, finance, sales, HR, operations, and similar

  • Department-specific demos and examples tied to real workflows
  • Quick wins for adoption in the next sprint
  • Advanced prompt engineering and workflow design
  • Identifying essential tools per department
  • Guided practice so participants ship one real output during the program

Outcome • Team-level adoption and efficiency

AI Builders

Enablement workshop + build sprint

AI-native teams or teams who have completed Essentials and Deep Dive

  • Hackathon-style sessions with your data and problem statements
  • Prototype real solutions alongside engineers
  • Custom builds for problems that off-the-shelf tools do not solve
  • API integrations and automation where it unlocks scale
  • AI safety and security practices for production-minded rollouts

Outcome • Working AI tools and prototypes you can iterate on
